Lauderdale Suzuki Group

Lauderdale Suzuki Group is a Suzuki programme run by a team of Suzuki violin teachers, musicianship teachers and pianists in North London. Founded by Elisabeth Waterhouse in 1978, we started with only about 30 children and have now expanded to around 80 families. The Group takes its name from the fine 17th century building, Lauderdale House, which is situated on the edge of Waterlow Park in Highgate. The Groups run every Wednesday during term time, from 3.55 pm until 7 pm. All children and parents learning in Lauderdale Groups also take a weekly individual lesson with a qualified Suzuki teacher.
